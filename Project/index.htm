Who?

My name is Alexandre and im the creator of this fun litle project called "The Backlogs". I'm currently studying web development and i have a deep love for games, maybe even creating them in a not so far future. We never know, right?

Why?

"The Backlogs" started as a fun little project for myself as a way to organize and finish my backlog of games that i stacked up over time but now it grew up into and organizing tool for game sessions and i intend to share it with everyone worldwide.

How?

Its Easy! You just choose your games and organize them in the order you want to play them or random to change it up, you choose also the time you are available to play and you click generate! Done and simple! Also you can have more than one scheadule if you want.
